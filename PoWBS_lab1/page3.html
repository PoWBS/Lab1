Frostpunk

This subpage is devoted to the game Frostpunk. Here is the official website.

Main premise

Frostpunk is a survival citybuilder set in harsh winter.
As a city leader, you have to be bold not to lean towards immoral choices, making your people suffer.

Game modes

Frostpunk has two main game modes:

Scenarios
Finite game modes focused on a particular goal.
Endless survival
As the name suggest, an endless mode focused purely on surviving and building your city
