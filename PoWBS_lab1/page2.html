The Competetive Scene

The competetive scene for the game is still going, though not as strong as its rival game Starcraft II

Still it manages to gather a noteable following, with pro players from all over the world playing against each other in different tournaments

Noteable players are the current number one in the wolrd TheViper from Norway and another top 10 player also from Norway MbL

Other noteable names are Liereyy, TaToH, Hera, JorDan, FatDragon, and Mr.Yo
